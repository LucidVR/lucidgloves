The printed models did not fit completely.

I faced the following problems:
• the hole for the nut is too small
• the slot in the potentiometer knob starts too high
• this leads to spring bending
• I still do not understand how to tie a thread on a spool

So I changed the files.
If the dimensions of your potentiometer are the same as mine, then this is what you need!

List of changes.
Holder: Added support for PERFECT printing.
Tensioner: Increased nut hole. The spring bed is raised. The height is increased accordingly.
New! Spacer: The part to be placed under the spring. You can change its height to better match your spring.
Cover: Changed the height to match the changes in other details. The holes for the thread attachment have been changed.

[Potentiometer.PNG] file: check if the dimensions of your potentiometer and mine are the same.

[Assembly.SKP] file: here you can see the assembly. Also, these are the sources of the models. You can change them if required. Open in SketchUP.

[Assembly.PNG] file: Assembly diagram.
